Amazing Facts About Santa Claus

Santa Claus is based on St. Nicholas, a 4th-century Greek bishop known for his generosity.
In many cultures, Santa is known by different names, such as Kris Kringle and Father Christmas.
Santa's sleigh is said to be pulled by eight reindeer, with Rudolph leading the way.
The tradition of hanging stockings comes from the story of St. Nicholas, who left gold coins in stockings hung by the fire.
Santa Claus is said to live at the North Pole, where he operates his toy factory with the help of elves.
